This is a system of seven alarms intended to give the user the best chance of having a lucid dream.

The first alarm is set for four hours after the alarm cycle is set. The idea is to interrupt your sleep at the point when you are the most tired.

The next alarms are are in minutes: 8, 4, 8, 12, 16, 20. The several short alarms are intended to give the user several opportunities to realize they are asleep.

The reset button resets to the beginning of the alarm cycle, in case the first alarm is interrupted and needs to be reset. The next button skips to the next alarm in the cycle. The stop button ends the alarm cycle.

This timer is best used with the FILD method. More information about this method and lucid dreaming in general can be found on reddit.com/r/luciddreaming.

More alarm presets and custom alarm options will be available in the future.
